SUCK
/sək/

verb
  1. draw into the mouth by contracting the muscles of the lip and mouth to make a partial vacuum.
    2. they suck mint juleps through straws
  2. be very bad, disagreeable, or disgusting.
    3. I love your country, but the weather sucks
noun
  1. an act of sucking something.
    2. the fish draws the bait into its mouth with a strong suck
